About Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a state in northern India. It is known for its beautiful mountainous landscape and its rich cultural heritage. Himachal Pradesh has a diverse population of over 7 million people, with several different ethnic groups and languages spoken. The capital of Himachal Pradesh is Shimla, which is a popular tourist destination.

Learn more about Himachal Pradesh

About Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a state in northern India, located mostly in the Himalayan mountains. It is known for its beautiful landscape, diverse culture, and rich history. Jammu and Kashmir has a population of over 12 million people, with several different ethnic groups and languages spoken. The capital of Jammu and Kashmir is Srinagar, which is famous for its beautiful lakes and gardens.

Temples in India

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

The Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is a famous Hindu temple located in the hill town of Tirumala in Andhra Pradesh. It is one of the wealthiest and most-visited temples in the world.

Kedarnath Temple

The Kedarnath Temple is a famous Hindu temple located in the Garhwal Himalayan range in Uttarakhand. It is one of the four Chota Char Dham pilgrimage sites and is dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple is a Bahá'í House of Worship located in New Delhi, India. It is known for its unique architecture and is one of the most-visited tourist attractions in India.

Nanda Devi
